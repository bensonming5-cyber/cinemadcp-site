FAQ

Common questions about DCP mastering and delivery.

These answers help clarify source formats, subtitles, KDM workflows, online delivery, and testing before ordering.

What is a DCP?

A Digital Cinema Package is the professional package format used for cinema playback in theaters and festivals.

Can you create DCP from MP4?

Yes. MP4 can often be used as a source if the quality, frame rate, audio, and aspect ratio are suitable.

Do you support subtitles?

Yes. Subtitle XML and burned-in subtitle versions can be prepared depending on the project requirements.

Do you support KDM?

KDM workflow support, certificate handling, and encrypted DCP assistance are available when needed.

How is delivery handled?

Delivery is usually handled online through secure transfer links such as Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer.

Can I test before ordering?

Yes. A free 10-second DCP test is available for suitable projects before full mastering.

What formats are accepted?

Common video, audio, subtitle, and existing DCP sources can be reviewed. Send file details for confirmation.
