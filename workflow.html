Workflow

A controlled process from source files to final delivery.

Each step is designed to reduce uncertainty, catch technical issues early, and keep cinema delivery predictable.

  1. Step 1

    Send files

    Upload your source file, existing DCP, subtitles, audio, and screening requirements.

  2. Step 2

    Technical review

    Source format, audio layout, subtitles, aspect ratio, frame rate, and delivery needs are reviewed.

  3. Step 3

    Free test DCP

    A short test package can be prepared so you can evaluate the workflow before full mastering.

  4. Step 4

    Client approval

    You confirm details, test results, and any final changes before production continues.

  5. Step 5

    Final DCP mastering

    The full DCP is prepared, checked, and validated for the agreed target workflow.

  6. Step 6

    Delivery

    The final package is delivered online with relevant notes, records, or encrypted workflow support when needed.
